The model runs entirely in-browser, with no data sent to external servers. All inference happens locally, on-device — fast, private, and accessible.

Drawing from a focused dataset of 4 ISL words, the system handles natural variation in hand size, lighting conditions, and signing style — with more signs to be added over time.
